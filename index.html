Reflections

January to March • 14 weeks of consistency

What changed

What started as a simple attempt to get moving slowly turned into a real routine. The biggest change was not just physical fitness, but becoming someone who shows up regularly, even on days when motivation is low.

Quarter 1 • Distance, discipline, momentum

What I learned

I learned that consistency matters more than perfection. Most runs were 5Ks, but the bigger win was building rhythm. The 10Ks and 15K were milestones, but the real success was returning again and again.

From April onward • Weekly updates

How this journal will continue

Instead of trying to recreate every early week in detail, this page will treat January to March as a first-quarter snapshot. From April onward, I will add short weekly notes with a few lines on what I did, how I felt, and what I noticed.
